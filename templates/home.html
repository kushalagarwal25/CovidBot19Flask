Covid-19 Chatbot to raise COVID-19 Awareness

The chatbot offers a wide range of information related to the COVID-19 pandemic in an easy-to-use conversational manner. This includes information about the coronavirus, its symptoms, and how it is transmitted; preventive measures that can be taken as a safeguard against the virus; health & travel advisories; and official government helplines for further information/helpdesk. It also provides links to additional resources for those who wish to learn more about the disease.

Features Of Our Covid-19 Chatbot

    Google trans library for Hindi to English and English to Hindi translation
    Pysymspell library for word sense disambiguation and sentence correction
    Semantic and statistic similarity for question answering system
    Bert and lstm for Intent Classification of question asked by user
    Flask as backend and HTML, CSS, Bootstrap and Javascript for frontent part of website
    Python Speech To Text library for Speech Recognition in our chatbot
